Kitfo - Ethiopian Beef Tartare

Prep: 30 mins
Cook: 10 mins
Yields: 4 Servings
Ethiopian Cuisine

01.

Start by finely mincing 500g of high-quality beef. Add it to a mixing bowl.

02.

Add 2 tablespoons of clarified butter, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of mitmita (Ethiopian spice blend) to the minced beef. Mix well.

03.

Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the seasoned beef mixture and cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring continuously.

04.

Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes. Kitfo is traditionally served raw or slightly cooked.
